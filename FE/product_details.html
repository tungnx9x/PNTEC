AP-530 Aerosol Fire Extinguisher

Aerosol fire extinguishers are a type of fire extinguisher used to extinguish or control fires. Aerosol extinguishers used in bus fire extinguishing systems are generally portable and easy to use. The chemicals they contain prevent the burning of oxygen by suffocating the fire or control the fire by stopping the reaction of flammable materials.

When aerosol fire extinguishers are activated, there is a patented safe cover design in the products as a precaution against physical damage due to the high pressure that may arise in the container.
